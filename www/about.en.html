¿Who makes Foxgame?


I am the main programer of all of this, I am Ramiro Aparicio Gallardo and I am a coder from Madrid who plays OGame daily since a couple of years and that likes to code things like this in his spare time. I make a small amount of money from this (just from the ads in this web and the board) but obviously I can not live from that money, it allows me to pay my web server (Foxgame board, Spanish OWiki, Universe and Server updates) and have some drinks from time to time.

If you want to know more about me or how Foxgame was created at first instance, read this interview made a couple of months ago by a polish player (translated to english):

http://board.ogame.org/thread.php?threadid=358114

But all of this will be impossible without you, the players, currently more than 150.000 player that have Foxgame installed in their Firefoxes (you can see detailed stats at: Mozilla.org ) and I just keep doing this just for you, becouse sometimes it is very difficult to get time to work in this but you keep asking for more, thanking the great job or just asking for a fix and I can not say no, so thank you all for the support.
